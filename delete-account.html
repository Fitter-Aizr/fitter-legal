Deleting Your Fitter Account

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Fitter account and what data is removed when you do. Fitter is operated by Zair AI (Zair Hicham Mehdi), based in Algeria.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Fitter account directly from within the app at any time. No email request or support ticket is required.

Step-by-step:
  1. Open the Fitter app on your device
  2. Sign in to your account if you are not already signed in
  3. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
  4. Scroll down to the Account section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data associated with your account will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your Fitter account, the following data is permanently and immediately removed from our systems:

  • Your account: email address, login credentials, and authentication records
  • Your profile data: username, age, gender, height, weight, body measurements, fitness goals, training preferences, avatar photo
  • All your scan data: scan history, physique scores, tier ratings, muscle breakdowns, archetype classifications, AI analysis results
  • All your uploaded photos: front-view physique photos from every scan, and any profile/avatar photos
  • Your generated plans: AI-generated workout plans and nutrition recommendations
  • Your social data: friendships, referral codes, and related records
  • Your activity data: daily check-ins, feature usage history

What Data May Be Retained

The following limited data may be retained after account deletion, only as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Payment records: subscription purchase records are retained by Apple App Store and Google Play Store according to their own retention policies. We do not store your payment details directly.
  • Anonymized aggregated data: we may retain anonymized analytics data that cannot be used to identify you personally
  • Legal compliance data: if required by applicable law (for example, records related to fraud prevention), we may retain specific minimum information for the legally-required duration

Subscription Cancellation

Deleting your Fitter account does not automatically cancel any active subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately through the platform where you purchased it:

  • iOS subscriptions: cancel through your Apple ID → Subscriptions in Settings
  • Android subscriptions: cancel through Google Play Store → Subscriptions

We recommend canceling your subscription before deleting your account to prevent further billing.

Alternative: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app (for example, if you lost access to your account), you can request account deletion by emailing us. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days.
